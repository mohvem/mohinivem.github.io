Given how much time we spend in front of the screen, our eyes are constantly strained, which manifests with reduced blinking rates. With that in mind, for Hack Arizona 2018, we built a solution to track a person's blinks through their webcam. If the person's number of blinks is below a threshold or too much time has elapsed, we printed a warning message and made the computer sleep to remind the person to take a break. It was rewarding to see how I was able to leverage existing skills to solve an important health problem, and we won Best Health for our efforts!
Tools:
Python, eye tracking technology
